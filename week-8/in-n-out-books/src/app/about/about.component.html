Joanne Rowling was born on July 31st, 1965, and she has a young sister who is two years younger than her. Joanne lived with her sister and parents in Winterbourne, Gloucestershire. She then moved to Tutshill, Chepstow, with her family. Joanne grew up surrounded by books because her parents loved reading. Joanne went to school at Wyedean Comprehensive school to study French and Classics at the University of Exeter. In 1997 she wrote her first book called Harry Potter and the Philosopher’s Stone and became a quick seller. Later on, she continued to write more books and received many awards and honors, including OBE for services to children’s literature.
